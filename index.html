About the Film

This 60 mins documentary film is a truth-seeking journey across India to verify / debunk the allegation of caste discrimination in our ancient temples. The method of enquiry is Truth, The Whole Truth & Nothing but the Truth. We visited all the 4 corners of India, documenting the current practices of ancient Indian temples vis a vis caste. Are Dalits not allowed in temples? Can non brahmins ever be priests?

Director

Nikhil Singh Rajputt


Nikhil Singh Rajputt was born in Chhapra-Bihar and completed his High School in Varanasi and his Secondary School in Ranchi. He prepared for his engineering from Kota-Rajasthan. He graduated from Indian Institute Of Technology [IIT] Delhi with a B.Tech in Textile Technology {2007}. He was an Assistant Director in the Mumbai Film Industry and later attended a Summer Program at the University Of Southern California [USC] Film School {2009}. His debut film was an Indie Bollywood feature 'With Love, Delhi! [2011]' which was released across theaters in India and starred Tom Alter, Seema Biswas, Kiran Kumar. He is also known for his award-winning documentaries.
